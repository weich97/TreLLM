clipped by `max_abs_weight`, and max gross exposure is scaled down. This is
structured risk feedback, not return evidence.

is higher, and the risk report is approved. That is consistent with de-risking

The supported claim is limited. The risk report acts like external supervision,
but this single pair of steps is not profitability evidence and does not prove
stable learning.

steps, more seeds, rolling windows, and a counterfactual run without structured
risk feedback. Without that, broader conclusions are unsupported.
